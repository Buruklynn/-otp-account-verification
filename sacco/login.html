LAINISHA SACCO

Lainisha Sacco is a member-driven financial cooperative offering savings and credit services. Our goal is to empower members financially by providing accessible, affordable, and innovative financial solutions.

We pool financial resources and offer responsive products and services that promote socio-economic growth. Guided by integrity, professionalism, and transparency, Lainisha Sacco helps members achieve financial independence.
