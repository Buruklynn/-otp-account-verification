Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is account reactivation?

Account reactivation is the process of restoring access to a previously dormant or inactive account.

2. How do I know if my account is dormant?

If you haven't used your account for a long time, it may be marked as dormant. Contact us to confirm the status.

3. How long does an account become dormant?

Typically, an account is considered dormant after 6 months of inactivity. However, the exact period may vary depending on the rules of your SACCO.

4. How can I reactivate my account?

To reactivate your account, you need to fill out a reactivation form and submit it with your valid identification and a small fee.

5. Do I need to visit the SACCO office to reactivate my account?

No, you can submit the reactivation request online through the website. The SACCO will verify your details and reactivate your account remotely.

6. What documents do I need to reactivate my account?

You will need to upload a copy of your national ID, along with your signature and a completed reactivation form.

7. How much is the reactivation fee?

The reactivation fee is KSH 150. This fee is required to complete the reactivation process.

8. How do I make the payment for reactivation?

You can make the payment through mobile money or bank transfer. Further details are provided during the reactivation process.

9. Can I reactivate my account without paying the fee?

No, the reactivation process cannot be completed without the payment of the required fee.

10. How long will it take to reactivate my account?

The reactivation process usually takes up to 3 business days, depending on the verification of your details.

11. What if I don’t remember my account number?

If you forget your account number, you can select the option "I don't remember my account number" during the reactivation process, and we'll assist you.

12. Can I reactivate a joint account?

Yes, joint accounts can also be reactivated by providing the necessary details of all account holders.

13. What happens if I don’t reactivate my dormant account?

If you don't reactivate your dormant account, it may be permanently closed after a certain period, and any remaining balance may be forfeited according to SACCO rules.

14. Can I withdraw from my dormant account before reactivation?

Withdrawals are not allowed from dormant accounts until they are reactivated.

15. What should I do if I face problems during the reactivation process?

If you face any issues, please contact our customer support for assistance, and we will guide you through the process.

16. How can I update my personal information before reactivating my account?

You can update your personal information by contacting SACCO customer service or updating it online during the reactivation process.

17. What happens after I submit the reactivation request?

Once your request is submitted, our team will verify your information, process the reactivation, and notify you once it's completed.

18. Is there a limit to the number of dormant accounts I can reactivate?

No, you can reactivate as many dormant accounts as you have, as long as you follow the required procedure for each account.

19. Can I change my SACCO branch after reactivating my account?

Yes, you can request to transfer your account to another branch after reactivation. Please contact customer service to initiate the transfer.

20. How do I know when my account is fully reactivated?

You will receive a confirmation message or email once your account has been reactivated successfully.
