Welcome to Lainisha Sacco Society Ltd

About Us: Lainisha Sacco Society Ltd, formerly known as Mwea Rice Farmers Sacco, was registered as an autonomous savings and credit organization on January 21, 1993. Over the years, we have been dedicated to enhancing the financial well-being of our members through a variety of financial services.

Our Vision: To be the leading and preferred financial solutions provider.

Our Mission: To pool financial resources and offer customers responsive products and services to promote their socio-economic growth through technological innovation and good corporate governance.
